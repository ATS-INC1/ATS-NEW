Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an ATS-approved carrier?

You can become an approved carrier by using the link below to request a carrier setup packet:

Become a Carrier

Where do you have loads?

ATS Logistics and Sureway Transportation Company work with thousands of shippers across the contiguous U.S. and Canada. All of this freight is available to approved carriers. 

Do you have a free load board service?

ATS Logistics has a free load board for approved carriers to use. This is accessible at brokeredloads.com.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes! You can use the ATS FreightMatch mobile application to search, find and select freight from your iOS or Android device. 

How will I receive payment?

Based on your preference, as a brokered carrier we can pay you by check or via direct deposit.

How quickly do you pay?

As long as you submit your invoice within 10 days of delivery, you can expect to be paid within 3-5 business days.
