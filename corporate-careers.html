Frequently Asked Questions About ATS Careers

What if I don't know which position to apply for?

That's okay! With so many opportunities to choose from, we don't blame you!

We recommend using the "Position Type" filter to narrow down which career categories pique your interest and align with your expertise.

Once you find an opportunity that aligns with your background, we invite you to submit an application. If your skills align, you'll be given an opportunity to chat with a member of the ATS talent acquisition team who can help you find the exact fit.

When can I expect a response on my application?

Once you properly submit your application, you should receive an immediate automated response via email. (If you don't receive that email within five minutes, please let us know)

If you're a good fit for the opportunity you applied for â€” or if our talent acquisition team thinks you fit a different opportunity â€” you'll hear from a team member within 1-3 business days.

Due to the high number of applications, we are unable to individually connect with every applicant.

What does the interview process look like?

While each step can vary slightly depending on the position you're considering, you can expect to go through seven key steps during the interview process:

  1. Submit an application for your desired position
  2. Discuss the opportunity with one of our talent acquisition specialists to learn more about the opportunity and how it aligns with your wants and needs
  3. Depending on the opportunity you applied for, you may be asked to do a one-way video interview
  4. Following a video interview, you may be asked to come into the office to go through an in-person interview with one or two hiring managers
  5. Depending on the opportunity you applied for, you may be asked to conduct a job shadow or an additional interview with different team members for more exposure to the duties of the role.
  6. If you're selected as the top choice for the opportunity, you'll be provided a contingent job offer â€” which means the job is yours pending a background and reference check!
  7. Upon acceptance, your day to start thriving with ATS will be set!

**Each step of the process is contingent on being qualified for the position**

What benefits does ATS offer its employees?

As an ATS employee, you'll get a competitive and comprehensive benefits package that focuses on your overall well-being. Benefits include things like medical, dental and vision plans, along with paid time off â€” plus tuition reimbursement and MORE!

Medical Insurance 

Health benefits and coverage programs are a staple piece of every benefits package. ATS employees have the choice between two comprehensive high-deductible health plans with the additional option to participate in a Health Savings Account (HSA).

Dental Insurance

Our affordable PPO (Preferred Provider Organization) plan offers employees 100 percent preventative coverage on basic services (some restrictions apply).

Life can be unpredictable and we want to ensure our employees are offered as many opportunities as possible to plan for the unexpected. Critical illness insurance, accident insurance and a hospital indemnity plan are offered as a voluntary benefit for self, spouse and/or child(ren).

Life insurance benefits provide financial support and stability to your family or listed beneficiaries should you pass away. Life insurance coverage is provided by ATS with the option to purchase additional coverage. Coverage for your spouse and children is also available.

Available to pre-tax medical/dental/vision insurance premiums and to reimburse eligible health and/or daycare expenses on a pre-tax basis. (Medical Flexible Spending Account: Eligible the 1st of the plan year following benefit effective date.)

Spending time away from work is important to your overall health and well-being, which is why we're proud to offer competitive PTO options based on tenure with ATS. PTO benefits start at 15 days in your first year of employment and increase as your employment with ATS grows.
